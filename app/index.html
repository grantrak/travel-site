Guiding in the backcountry for over 15 years.

Ever since a young age, Bob Johnson has been sharing his love for adventuring and the outdoors.

Here’s how he got started…

A native to the Front Range of Colorado, Bob Johnson began skiing at age 6. Since then, he has skied in North America, South America, Europe, and Asia. In his riding, Bob has primarily focused on skiing all of the 14,000ft peaks in Colorado. As an alpinist, he has summited Denali, climbed classic routes in Rocky Mountain National Park, the Canadian Rockies, and the Alps. Bob made the first descent of a 17,000-foot peak in a seldom visited corner of the Indian Himalayas. He was also selected to participate in the first Alpine Mentors Program, started by world-class alpinists to train and mentor the next generation of alpine climbers.

As a guide, Bob loves to teach modern techniques for skiing safely and efficiently. He has experience coaching and training skiers who want to maximize their performance and used these skills to lead the Mountain High School skiing team to a state victory. His work has taken him up and down the Front Range of Colorado to the classic backcountry skiing of the San Juan’s, and the world-class steeps of Alaska. Bob’s calm demeanor and expertise are ever present whether it is sharing his enthusiasm for cskiing with a beginner or working with an advanced skiier looking to up their game and avalanche knowledge.

Terrain Options

Cameron Pass

All skill levels

Cameron Pass is a very remote mountain pass in northern Colorado, known for it's seclusion and deep snow. Tours for all skill levels can be found here. From mellow bowls and glades, to steep chutes and tight trees, Cameron pass is sure to please those just getting into the sport and seasoned veterans!

Indian Peaks

Advanced only

The stunning backdrops and steep lines of the Indian Peaks Wilderness come at a price. Long approaches and rough weather make this the perfect area for advanced skiers to put their skills to the test. If you're willing to brave the elements, the Indian Peaks are sure to reward you!

Berthoud Pass

All skill levels

With an incredible amount of terrain choices and easy access, Berthoud Pass is a great choice for anyone. Being close to Denver, it is a very popular option for most skiers on the Front Range. An old abandoned ski resort makes the perfect training ground for newcomers to the sport.
